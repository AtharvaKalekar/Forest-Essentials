Ayurvedic Hair Care

In ancient India, special emphasis was given to ayurvedic hair care since beautiful hair has been prized throughout history. Ancient Indian texts have always depicted women with luxurious tresses.

For these hair treatments, apprentices would collect the ingredients prescribed by an Ayurvedic physician, including leaves of the Brahmi plant, Jatamansi (or spikenard), Amla, Bhringraj, and Japapatti – that were and still are considered essential for the health of hair.
