How to Use

For best results, follow these steps:

  1. Cleanse your face thoroughly with a gentle cleanser.
  2. Apply toner to balance your skin's pH levels.
  3. If using a serum, apply it before the night cream.
  4. Take a small amount of the Transformative Soundarya Night Cream on your fingertips.
  5. Gently dot the cream on your forehead, cheeks, chin, and neck.
  6. Using upward and outward motions, massage the cream into your skin until fully absorbed.
  7. For enhanced results, use daily as part of your nighttime skincare routine.

Pro Tip: For an intensive treatment, apply a slightly thicker layer once a week as an overnight mask.
